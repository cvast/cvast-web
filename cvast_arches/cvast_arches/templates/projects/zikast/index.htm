Zika virus early warning system

Florida, Puerto Rico, and Brazil

A collaboration between Prof. Ryan Carney (USF), Prof. Sean Ahearn (CUNY), and several international institutions, this predictive model will build upon the team's previously successful results predicting epidemics of West Nile and dengue viruses. Real-time Zika risk maps will be generated and supplied to local agencies in Florida, Puerto Rico, and Brazil, in order to target mosquito control efforts.

West Nile virus early warning system (red=high risk, black=human cases).

From: Carney et al 2011 Emerging Infectious Diseases
