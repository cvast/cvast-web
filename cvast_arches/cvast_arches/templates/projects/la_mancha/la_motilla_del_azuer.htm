La Motilla del Azuer

La Mancha, Spain

The Motilla del Azuer is a Bronze Age fortified farming community in La Mancha, Spain. Motillas are organized around deep wells, and are fortified to project access to water.

The site is contributing to our research on fortifications, warfare, and water control systems.
