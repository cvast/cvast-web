Title

A collaboration with Prof. Sean Ahearn (CUNY) and several international institutions, this predictive model will build upon our previously successful results predicting epidemics of West Nile and dengue viruses. Real-time risk maps will be generated and supplied to local agencies in Florida, Puerto Rico and Brazil, in order to target mosquito control efforts.
